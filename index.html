About NIT Silchar

National Institute of Technology Silchar (NIT Silchar) is one of the premier technical institutes in India. Established in 1967, it offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, science, and management disciplines. The campus is known for its lush greenery, advanced laboratories, vibrant student clubs, and numerous workshops and events throughout the year. NIT Silchar encourages innovation, research, and holistic development for its students. Feel free to contact the administration for any assistance!!!!

NITS LIBRARY

Welcome to the NITS Library! Our library houses thousands of books across multiple disciplines including Engineering, Science, Technology, Management, and Humanities. It offers a quiet study environment, digital resources, and a rich collection of research papers.

Ground Floor

The ground floor hosts General Reference books, Engineering Textbooks, and a Computer Lab. Sections are organized alphabetically by subject for easy access. Comfortable reading areas are available near the windows.

First Floor

The first floor contains Management, Humanities, and Social Sciences sections. There are private study cubicles, journal archives, and multimedia resources for student projects and research.

Second Floor

The second floor hosts Science & Technology books, Research papers, and Thesis archives. Collaborative study rooms and discussion zones are available for group learning.
